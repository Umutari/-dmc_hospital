🔄 KEY WORKFLOWS:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PATIENT SIGNUP WITH INSURANCE:
  1. Patient goes to /dmc/signup.php
  2. Fills form including insurance provider selection
  3. Submits → Account created with insurance linked
  4. First invoice → Insurance claim auto-created
  5. Payment collected → Breakdown shown (insurance % vs patient %)

RECEPTIONIST REGISTERS PATIENT:
  1. Receptionist → Register Patient
  2. Fills form with insurance dropdown (shows coverage %)
  3. Submits → Patient created with insurance
  4. Works exactly like self-signup

ACCOUNTANT TOPUPS PATIENT ACCOUNT:
  1. Accountant → Topup Money
  2. Search for patient
  3. Enter amount & reason
  4. Click "Add Credit & Generate Receipt"
  5. Receipt displays with all details
  6. SMS sent to patient automatically
  7. Print receipt for records

ACCOUNTANT COLLECTS PAYMENT:
  1. Accountant → Payments
  2. Select invoice
  3. Show payment amount → Auto-calculates insurance breakdown
  4. Insurance box shows real-time split
  5. Collect payment → Insurance claim auto-created
  6. SMS receipt sent with breakdown
